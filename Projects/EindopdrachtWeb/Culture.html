What about culture in Amsterdam?

Bikes in Amsterdam

In Amsterdam a lot of the people who live there ride their bike around town. They are almost forced to because of all the very small and crowded roads. Some even park their car outside the city and reach it with their bikes, because they spare more time than to drive through the city. Because of all the bikes their is also a lot of bikethefts taking place in Amsterdam

Red light district

Amsterdam is also famous for another, rather unpleasant (not to some) exhibition which is the redlight district. These are really small corridors with lots of women on both sides. You can pay these women for sex. A lot of people are against these sort of things, just like the drug use. But these women are not treated in a right manner , there is actually something like a pimp, and they aren't nice people.
