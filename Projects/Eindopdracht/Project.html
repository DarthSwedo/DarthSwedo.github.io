My website

This is my project for Web. I’m 20 years old and I study New Media And Communication Technology at Howest. My project is about Amsterdam. Why? Because i think it is one of the most beautiful cities in the world. It’s a very peaceful city with little trafic and lots of things to see. The mentality is different in Amsterdam. People don’t care about how you look or what you do. They’re just happy about who you are, and that’s the most important thing in life!
