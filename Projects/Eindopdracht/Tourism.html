Activities & excursions

There are countless ways to see Amsterdam - including by bike, boat, bus or foot - as well as endless activities like sailing, museum tours or a windmill excursion, to suit every taste. A tour or excursion is a great way to discover Amsterdam, and gives you the chance to see and experience a side of the Netherlands' capital that you might never see.

Clubbing in Amsterdam

When it comes to nightlife in Amsterdam, there are venues, dance festivals and club nights to suit every taste. DJs keep the crowd going with everything from techno, dub step and trance to hip-hop, funk and deep house. Some of the most well-known venues include Paradiso, Melkweg, Bitterzoet, Club Up, Trouw, AIR and Escape.

Museums & galleries in Amsterdam

Amsterdam is home to many of the world's most famous museums. No trip to the city is complete without (at least) a visit to the Rijksmuseum, Van Gogh Museum, Stedelijk Museum or Anne Frank House. The city also has a multitude of treasures outside of the Museum District. From unusual museums about cats to an amazing collection of handbags, plus photography, archaeology and more, Amsterdam has museums and galleries to suit every type of interest.
